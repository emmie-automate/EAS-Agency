Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before enrolling.

Do I need technical experience to join?
No. This course is built for complete beginners as well as experienced marketers. If you can send an email and use WhatsApp, you have enough technical skill to follow along. We start from the basics in Week 1.
How do I pay? Can I pay in Naira?
All prices are in USD and accepted via card, bank transfer, or PayPal. If you prefer to pay in Naira, send us a WhatsApp message and we will arrange it at the current exchange rate. No one is turned away due to payment method.
What if I miss a live session?
Every session is recorded and shared within 24 hours. You will not fall behind. That said, showing up live is strongly encouraged — the Q&A and live demos are where most of the learning happens.
What tools do I need? Will I have to pay for them?
We use ChatGPT (free), Claude (free), Canva (free), Buffer or Metricool (free), Mailchimp or Brevo (free), and HubSpot CRM (free). You can complete the entire course at zero additional cost. Paid upgrades are optional and only worth it once you have outgrown the free tiers.
What is the difference between Starter and Full Cohort?
Starter gives you all the recordings, templates, and prompts to work through at your own pace. Full Cohort puts you in the live sessions — you build everything in real-time, get direct feedback, and join the accountability group. Most students see better results from the live experience.
When does the next cohort start?
Cohort dates are announced via the EAS Agency WhatsApp channel and email list. Enrol now to secure your spot and you will receive the start date and session schedule immediately after payment.
