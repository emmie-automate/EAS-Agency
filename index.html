+
I work with clinics and healthcare providers, coaches and consultants, founders and startup teams, and local businesses that want to automate operations and grow their digital presence. I've delivered for clients in Nigeria, USA, Thailand, and the UK.
How long does a project take?
+
Most automation builds and CRM setups are complete within 2–4 weeks. Full growth strategy packages run on 90-day cycles. Social media management and ad campaigns are ongoing monthly engagements. I always agree on a clear timeline upfront.
Do you work remotely?
+
Yes — 100% remote, globally. I work across completely different time zones and deliver everything online. My current active clients are based in Texas (USA), London (UK), Lagos (Nigeria), and Thailand.

We build systems, not just campaigns. We combine AI automation, CRM setup, content, and advertising into one connected growth engine — so every part of your business works together, not in silos.

Yes. All systems come with a 2-week handover and full documentation. For ongoing management, I offer monthly retainer packages covering social media, ads, CRM maintenance, and automation monitoring.
