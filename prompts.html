How to use these prompts

5 Rules That Change Everything

The prompt is only 20% of the result. Here is the other 80%.

Replace every [bracket]
Fill placeholders with real business details before sending. The more specific, the better the output.
Paste your brand voice guide first
Before any writing task, paste your brand voice guide at the top of the chat. One habit, massive improvement.
Iterate — never accept draft one
After the first output, send follow-up prompts: 'Make it more direct.' 'Cut by 30%.' '5 alternative hooks.' The skill is in the iteration.
Try both ChatGPT and Claude
Same prompt, different output. ChatGPT is faster for ideation. Claude is stronger for nuance and tone. Use both.
Save your winning prompts
When a prompt produces something you would actually use — save it. After 30 days you have a personal prompt library.
